What it means to rescue computers

These days, nearly everyone has a computer or laptop lying around, not being used to it's full potential. Maybe it's too slow, or has trouble launching your favorite applications. Maybe you've been meaning to upgrade to something faster, but are hesitant to spend nearly a thousand dollars for a new computer. Read below to see how Hardware Rescue can help!

At Hardware Rescue we install the best Free and Open Source (FOSS) software available on your computer. We can replace your current operating system (typically Microsoft Windows) with a completely free alternative.

This will improve your computers performance, limit your vulnerability to malware and viruses, and with no forced or paid updates. Your computer can run like new with a little help from Hardware Rescue!
Let us evaluate your computer and consult with you on the best course of action: repair or replace. Many problems can be fixed by replacing a single component, and with older hardware, they are usually pretty inexpensive. If we can, we'll get your old computer working like new, saving you money.

Sometimes however, the cost to bring an aging computer back to life is just too high. In that case, we can offer a fantastic, modern computer that we've already rescued, at a fraction of the price new.
We seek out schools and businesses that are upgrading their gently-used computer systems and buy them for pennies on the dollar. Then we refurbish them, configure them with the best free software, and sell them to you at an unbeatable price!

Since all the software is all free, you only pay for our time and effort ensuring everything is working smoothly. Also, every rescued computer comes with three months of free support: We'll be here to help if you're having trouble!
